TRACK

TRACK is a set of templates in the Public Library of SafetyCulture, an app for your phone. Supervisors use it to assess staff performance in-vivo. Staff use it to practice. Optional e-sign, geolocation, take pictures of forms/performance, share PDF reports. TRACK is not "one more thing." It's ten less things for a better result.

TRACK
